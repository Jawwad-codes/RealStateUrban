FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

Our team responds to all inquiries within 2 business hours during office hours. For urgent matters, call us directly.

Do I need to be pre-approved before touring homes?

We recommend getting pre-approved before touring, but it's not required. We can connect you with trusted Austin lenders if needed.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of Austin and the surrounding metro including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Buda, Kyle, and Westlake.

Is there a fee to work with a buyer's agent?

In most cases, buyer's agent fees are covered by the seller. We'll explain the current structure clearly before we start working together.

Can you help with investment properties?

Absolutely. Carlos Reyes leads our investment division and can provide cap rate analysis, cash flow projections, and market comparisons.

Do you work with out-of-state buyers?

Yes — Emily Chen specializes in remote and relocation buyers. We offer virtual tours, video walkthroughs, and remote closing coordination.

Prefer to call?

Our main line is staffed Monday–Friday 9am–6pm and Saturday 10am–4pm.
