Our Story

Born in Austin, built for Austin

UrbanNest Realty was founded in 2013 by Sarah Mitchell, a lifelong Austinite who saw a gap in the market: buyers and sellers deserved a boutique experience with big-brokerage resources.

We started with a small team of three agents on Congress Ave. Today, we're a team of 20+ specialists covering every corner of the Austin metro — from the luxury estates of Westlake to the vibrant condos of East 6th Street.

What hasn't changed is our commitment: every client gets a dedicated agent, transparent pricing, and the kind of local knowledge you can only get from people who actually live here.
